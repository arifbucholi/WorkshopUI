Our Story

The term JDM, which stands for Japanese Domestic Market, refers to cars produced by official manufacturers exclusively for the domestic Japanese market. Typically, these car models boast extremely high performance and speed, making them not necessarily street legal if marketed to other countries.

Hence, countries like Indonesia find it challenging to easily obtain JDM cars. The only option is to modify existing vehicles with JDM spare parts. Fortunately, JDM car parts are not limited to Japanese-made cars; they can also be installed on JDM-styled cars with similar models. Consequently, modifications of this kind are referred to as the JDM-look.
